Oakwell

"Oakswell that ends well"

Description

Oakswell is one of the oldest members of the Mirage Crew, and an old friend of Mirage, and a caretaker of the forest. Oakwell is a very big fan of the Zen Garden, and almost always stays there. Oakwell is a swampy creature, made of mud, and tree bark. Oakwell swears to defend the forest with his life, and anyone who messes with it, or Oakwell's friends, will feel Oakwells wrath. Oakwell is one of 5 Holos who keep a pet, with Oakwells pet being Flowo. Oakwell is the reason Flowo even exists; when Oakwell kept a special flower so safe, it gained sentience. Oakwell premiers in episode 24, "Swampy Adventures". Oakwell can be described as loyal, caring, and overprotecting, yet fierce and aggressive to anyone who harms the environment or his friends. Oakwell is closest to: Flowo, Mirage, Grekka, and Reef, and dislikes: Kole and Clover.

Ability

Name: Thorn Trap

Bring Brambles up out of the ground to make a trap. These traps last 120 seconds and affect any Holo or Animatronic who steps on it. Takes 5 seconds to activate after using the ability. Cooldown: 60s.

Passive

Name: Heart of Oak

Oakwell heals one heart every 8 nights.

Pinedo

Description

Pinedo doesn't like to be seen; often described as shy, she is rarely seen with the Mirage Crew. She's always nervous about losing her head, quite literally. Being detached from her body, she uses her third arm to hold onto it. Pinedo premiered in episode 36 - "Careful what you wish for". Pinedo doesn't like coming up on stage often, but tends to be made to on weekend shows. She is often described as shy, anxious, and nervous, and shows kids it's okay to be worried, as she gets over it in every episode she appears in. Pinedo is friends with Rubix, and Ace, but dislikes [Name null].

Ability

Name: Detached Head

This Holo can throw her head away from animatronics, making them lose interest in her. However, she becomes a sitting duck, waiting for her body to come get her. Cooldown: 50s.

Passive

Name: Headless Fear

This Holo drops her head for 3 seconds when seen for the first time each night, making her unable to move for this period.

Chester

Description

Chester is not one to say much unless it aids him, and is quite a greedy one, always fighting over anything, be it bottlecaps or gold. Chester never shares and often upsets a lot of the other Holos. Chester is quite cranky and has been around the Mirage Crew since episode 1, but didn't get his first episode staring him until episode 33 - "The Bottlecap Thief.". Chester is easily described as rude, greedy, and ignorant, and is a good example of what not to be, as Chester never learns to share. Chester likes Pinedo as he sees her as a free source of things, and dislikes Flash and Lanberd, who keep trying to stop Chester from stealing.

Ability

Name: Disguise

Chester can tuck in his arms, disguising himself as a prop for 5 seconds, making all animatronics lose interest in him. Cooldown: 60s.
